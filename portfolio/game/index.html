design a mobile app/game for a commuter traveling to work/college. I decided the game should be short and simple with easy controls for a mobile/tablet device enabling it to be played whilst commuting.


The levels are short with a twist as each level get noticeably harder. After receiving feedback I removed the grass texture (.png) to give the game a minimal aesthectic appearance. Initially I included birds swooping down for the player to kill/attack however this proved difficult to do, bearing in mind the player hold the device while he/she is travelling.
