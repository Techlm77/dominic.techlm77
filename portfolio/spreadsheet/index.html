Spreadsheet

I was ask to produced a spreadsheet for the payroll for Gateshead college. I created a colour coded spreadsheet which takes into an account the different pay level, student loan and car parking.


Each row shows the payroll number, name, their position, rate of pay, student loan and car parking. The spreadsheet is fully automatic making it user friendly whilst doing an effiecient job.


I also produced four different charts showing the output of the spreadsheet.
